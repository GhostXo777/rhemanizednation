The Guiding Hand

Meet the Visionaries and Stewards

These leaders carry the mantle for the Young Revivalist Academy (YRA) Vision, setting the apostolic direction, and ensuring every young revivalist is equipped to transform their sphere of influence.

John Victor Solution Rhema

General Overseer & Visionary Commander [G.O.C]

As the General Overseer and Visionary Commander (G.O.C) of The YRA Vision, Solution Rhema carries a profound apostolic mandate to ignite global revival. With an unyielding passion for Christ and strategic insight, he leads the movement, setting the prophetic trajectory for raising young, uncompromised revivalists destined to transform nations. His leadership is marked by a deep commitment to spiritual purity, kingdom advancement, and equipping a generation for divine purpose.

"I Am A Solution On Two Legs Walking."

📷 📸

Samuel Johnson

Y.R.A President - "Mr. Authority"

Samuel Johnson, affectionately known as 'Mr. Authority,' serves as the dynamic President of the Young Revivalist Academy (Y.R.A.). He is a seasoned teacher of the Word, renowned for his revelational and prophetic insights into God's word, rightly dividing the word of truth by Grace. Samuel's primary focus is to instill hunger, passion, reverence, and a deep understanding of spiritual dominion in Y.R.A. students, empowering them to walk in their God-given mandate and exert the kingdom across every sphere of society. He is a passionate advocate for rigorous spiritual development and practical application.

"Every man's manifestation is a result of their preparation, The building you see out is a type and shadow of the foundation concealed, strive for growth."

📷 📸

Enitan Segun Samuel

Y.R.A Administrator - "Anointed Rhema"

Enitan Segun Samuel, recognized as 'Anointed Rhema,' is a pivot Administrator for the Young Revivalist Academy (Y.R.A.). With a heart dedicated to nurturing young hearts, he oversees the operational excellence within the academy. He is a steadfast guide, committed to fostering an environment where revivalists can truly thrive and experience personal and corporate transformation.

"Intercession Is Loving Humanity On Our Knees."

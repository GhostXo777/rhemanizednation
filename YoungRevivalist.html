Our Apostolic End-Time Commission

We are commissioned in the army of Zion with an Apostolic End-Time Mandate: to bring Revival in Africa, positioning the Nation of God in Nigeria and in every African country. This move starts from the shores of Lagos and advances globally. *(Matthew 10:7-8)*

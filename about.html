Our Story, Our Mandate

We Stand In The Gap.

Rhemanized Nation is a movement of young revivalists, believing that young people hold the key to unlocking a global revival.

Explore Our Vision →

Our Foundation & Commitment

Our organization is dedicated to providing a safe and supportive environment for youths to grow, thrive, and understand their identity and purpose. We commit to enabling youths and teenagers to develop their talents and skills in an atmosphere that allows them to contribute meaningfully to society, shaping a better future for all in different spheres of influence.

Our Goal (Obadiah 1:21)

To raise young agents of change (young revivalists) in various spheres of influence from nation to nation.

Our Apostolic Vision & Commission

💪

Disciple-Making & Global Transformation

We specialize in disciple-making, raising young revivalists for global transformation. We serve as agents of change across nations. (Matt 28:19-20)

📚

Liberating from Spiritual Ignorance

We are dedicated to liberating children, youth, and teenagers from spiritual ignorance, equipping students with consciousness of their identity and authority in Christ Jesus.

🙏

Home for Intercessors & Worshippers

We are a home for passionate intercessors and apostolic worshippers, nurturing evangelists and influencing various spheres of society with the gospel. (Ezekiel 22:30)

What We Do: Catch Them Young

We understand the importance of reaching youth early, before they are influenced by the world's values. We dedicate ourselves to holistic development.

The Aim of Rhemanized Nation

  • Cultivate a new generation of global missionary intercessors (24/7 intercession and worship).
  • Fight spiritual ignorance and promote gospel education.
  • Equip the youth for purpose discover and advocate a God-fearing society.
  • Nurture a spiritually healthy generation through gospel films, apostolic worship, and outreach missions
